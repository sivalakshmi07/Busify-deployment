\chapter{INTRODUCTION}

With the rapid advancement of information technology and the widespread availability of the internet, traditional ticket booking systems have gradually evolved into digital platforms. Transportation services, especially bus travel, continue to play a vital role in connecting cities and towns, particularly in regions where buses remain the most economical and accessible mode of transport. However, conventional bus ticket booking methods often involve manual processes, long waiting times, lack of real-time seat availability, and limited flexibility for passengers.

This project is undertaken to address these challenges by providing a structured, user-friendly, and efficient web-based platform for booking bus tickets. The platform is designed to improve transparency, accessibility, and interaction between passengers and system administrators while ensuring organized data management and secure user authentication. 

By implementing modern web technologies like the MERN stack and a modular system architecture, the project aims to demonstrate how technology can enhance booking efficiency, reduce manual effort, and provide a scalable foundation for future improvements in digital transportation solutions.
