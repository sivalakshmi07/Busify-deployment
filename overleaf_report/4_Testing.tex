\chapter{TESTING}

Software testing is an essential phase in the software development life cycle. It ensures the application runs effectively, meets its architectural requirements, and successfully prevents logical race conditions such as Double Booking.

\section{Unit Testing}
Unit testing involves verifying individual components or functions of the Bus Ticket Booking platform independently.
